"Elden Ring" is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. Set in a sprawling, dark fantasy world created in collaboration with acclaimed novelist George R.R. Martin, the game invites players to explore the Lands Between, a realm ruled by demigods and fractured by the shattered Elden Ring. As a Tarnished, exiled and summoned back to restore the Elden Ring, players traverse vast landscapes, daunting dungeons, and formidable enemies. The game features deep lore, intricate world-building, and challenging combat, combining FromSoftware's signature gameplay with an open-world experience. Players can customize their characters, master various combat styles, and uncover hidden secrets in a richly detailed and atmospheric environment.
